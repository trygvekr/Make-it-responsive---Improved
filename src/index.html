Welcome to Inova

Inova is a newly formed company focused on frugal innovation. We arrange innovation camps to spread
awareness around our goals and philosophy, these events are a great way for startups to build connections
and become more aware of simplicity in design.

Frugal innovation or frugal engineering is the process of reducing the complexity and cost of a good and its production.
Here at Inova we aim to create simple products that are sustainable and easy to both produce and use. We
care about accessibility and universal design. Our products and events are made with this in mind.
